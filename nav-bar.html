About Dr. YS Babu Trust Clinic

Dr. YS Babu Trust Clinic is a leading fertility clinic in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. Over 15,000+ couples have successfully conceived through Dr. Babu's unique treatment approach, even after repeated failures with IUI, IVF, or surgeries.

Dr. Babu specializes in achieving natural pregnancy in complex cases such as Azoospermia, Bilateral Tubal Block, PCOS, and Varicocele – without surgery or IVF. His treatment uses only English medicines and is highly effective.
